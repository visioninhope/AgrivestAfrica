About Us





Who We Are

Our company, AgrivestAfrica, is a youth centered social enterprise that creates passive income for investors and maximize production for farmers. From a wide range of crop produce to choose from, our platform creates an open market place for buyers and sellers so that everyone involved in our Agri-business chain can benefit. At AgrivestAfrica, we are directly involved in the promotion of the SDGs such as 1, 2 , 8 and 9. We seek to change the narrative of making agriculture in Africa a business of providing subsistence but rather a sustainable and scalable business of profit and partnerships. We believe everyone should be involved in the agri-business chain in whatever way or form. As such, the AgrivestAfrica platform creates the opportunity to the general public to participate in the agri-business by partnering with farmers and aggregators directly for seasonal equity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the agricultural sector by providing framework, public investments, and support services needed to reform the agricultural value chain .

Our Vison

Our vision is to continue to grow as a leading web-based agro-industrial actor, providing innovative solutions that generate value all along our supply-chains, from countries of origin to final customers.
